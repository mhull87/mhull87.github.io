How's The Weather Today

Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide the current weather conditions and future weather predictions of Preston, UT, Soda Springs, UT, and Fish Haven, UT to people interested. There will be a home page with an overview of the current weather story for each town. Each town will have its own page and each town page will have further information on the weather and other town information and relevant articles.
