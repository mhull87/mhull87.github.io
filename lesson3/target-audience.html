Target Audience

People looking for updates on the weather in Preston, Soda Springs, or Fish Haven, Utah.

Persona 1:

Zachary Smith is a 33 year old engineer working for a small local company. He travels to different locations within the Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven community. He is interested in what the weather will be like and when possible storms will be coming through that could interrupt his work. He will be using a desktop computer as a viewport to check the weather before driving our to work.

Persona 2:

Shawna Roble is an 17 year old senior in high school living with her parents. She loves the outdoors. She is planning a hiking trip with friends. She is interested in what the weather will be like to plan the best day and time of day to go hiking. She will be using her Apple iPhone X as a viewport because she uses her phone for everything.

Senarios:

  1. Will there be a storm today that will prevent me from unloading my equiptment at a certain time?
  2. Will it be too warm to go hiking at 3pm?
  3. What is the average temperature for Preston, Utah in the Spring?
  4. Are there any storms predicted for next weekend when we go camping?
  5. What is the high temperature going to be today in Fish Haven?
