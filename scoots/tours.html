Tours

City Tour

This half day tour of the island starts off with a ride through the city to experience the local culture and beautiful architecture. Next we are headed down the coast for a stop on the peer to enjoy some fishing and local beach fun. We will stop for lunch in downtown Cozumel at a local resturant where you can enjoy authentic mexican food.

Myan Ruins Tour

This full day tour takes you deep into the central island to some amazing ruins! Enjoy the beauty of the Tulum archaelogical site and be ready to see some wildlife along the way.

Fishing Tour

This full day tour takes you down the coast to experiance some of the best fishing spots on the island. There are 5 fishing spots to stop and a whole lot of culture to take in.
